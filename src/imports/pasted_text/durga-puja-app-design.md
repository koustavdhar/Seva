Design a modern mobile application UI/UX for a festival utility app for Durga Puja in Kolkata, India.

The purpose of the app is to help millions of festival visitors easily find essential services while visiting Durga Puja pandals, especially:

• nearest public toilets
• police assistance booths
• emergency / medical centers
• transport routes to reach them

The app should prioritize speed, clarity, safety, and ease of navigation in crowded environments.

App Concept

Durga Puja is the largest festival in Kolkata, where millions of people roam across the city visiting decorated pandals (temporary temples).

Due to heavy crowds, visitors often struggle to find:

• nearby public toilets
• police booths for safety
• first aid / emergency centers
• transport options (metro, bus, auto, taxi)

The app acts as a festival navigation and safety assistant.

Target Users
